Police Killings in USA 2015

This is a visualisation that is intended to inform viewers about police killings on USA in 2015. The dataset includes victim's name, age, race, city, state, and etc.

Police Killings based on Months

From the graph we can conclude that police killings mostly Occurs in March with 114 cases recorded followed by April, January, February, May and lastly June with only 2 cases recorded.

Cause of Death based on Race/Ethnicity

From the stacked bar chart, most of the victims was shot to death followed by Taser, strucked by vehicle, death in custody, and few unknown cause of death.
